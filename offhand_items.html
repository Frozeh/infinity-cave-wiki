Mobs and offhand items

Every time a mob spawns it will be assigned a randomized offhand item, offhand items grant the mob special abilities listed below:

Wind Charge

Every 25 ticks, there is a 15% chance and 5% increase per tier for the mob to throw a wind charge at itself or at the player.

Firework Rocket

Every 5 seconds, there is a 50% chance for the mob to ride a firework rocket towards the player.

Golden Apple

Every 2.5 seconds there is a 15% chance and 5% increase per tier for the mob to eat a Golden Apple, where commons and uncommons have 1 Golden Apple, while rares have 2, epics have 3 and legendaries have 4.

Chorus Fruit

Every time the mob gets hit there is a 12.5% chance for the mob to eat a Chorus Fruit, teleporting randomly around the player. Commons and uncommons have 1 Chorus Fruit, while rares have 2, epics have 3 and legendaries have 4.

Golden Sword

10% increased Attack Damage, also increases ranged damage.

Leather Boots

10% increased Movement Speed.

Shield

+5 Armor.

Totem of Undying

Mob has a Totem of Undying, preventing them from death, can only spawn on higher tiers.

Tipped Arrows

Only Skeletons and Pillagers can have these, which have certain status effects on their arrows
