Deeprock Caverns

Deeprock Caverns generates between Sulfide Caves and Molten Caves, similar to Deep Dark, has a spaghetti-like cave generation, known for its blackstone patches and white calcite pillars.

Structures

Deeprock Caverns like Deep Dark only has one structure, the Dinosaur, which has a single barrel in the middle, which could contain enchanted golden apples, enchantlets and enchanted books.
