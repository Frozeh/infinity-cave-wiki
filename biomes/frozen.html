Frozen Caves

Generates between Amethyst Caves and Deep Dark, is a very rugged with many pillars and tons of packed and blue ice. Has a special plant one can use on bows to cause deadly ice explosions.

Structures

Frozen Caves may generate 5 different structures, the trial spawners and the Ancient Robot. A white, Yeti-like structure that contains two diamond blocks and a lot of iron, and three barrels. Be careful however, as this structure is trapped with a lot of TNT. Its loot may contain enchantlets, enchanted golden apples, enchanted books and ice sparks.

Frostpale Plant

A special plant that grows only in Frozen Caves, at max age it drops Frostpale Essence and Frostpale Plant, which may be replanted, but will only drop Frostpale if in Frozen Caves, also grows 2.5x faster in Frozen Caves. Frost Essence can be crafted with a bow to make the bow Frostpaled, which causes the next arrow to be a deadly ice bomb that detonates 5 seconds after hitting the ground, can only be used once.
