Limestone Grotto

Limestone Grotto generates between Amethyst Caves and Sulfide Caves, is very rugged and filled with lots lakes and rivers, where it is possible to fish for a Riptide IV trident, and dripstone-type blocks.

Structures

Limestone Grotto may generate 6 structures, 2 of which are biome-specific. The trial spawners may spawn here, Limestone Houses and Limestone Village. Limestone Houses may contain shulker shells and is extremely useful for inventory management, this structure contains a lot of functional blocks such as grindstone, smithing table, stonecutter etc. Limestone Village is a huge village with blacksmiths, tall houses, libraries etc. it is possible to find bundles, villagers, diamond armor, and other essentials.
