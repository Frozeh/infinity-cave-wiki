Deep Dark

Generates between Frozen Caves and Mushroom Gorge, has the same generation as Deeprock Caverns, a spaghetti-like pattern. It is one of the rarest biomes, slightly less rare than Mushroom Gorge.

Structures

Deep Dark only has one structure, the Deep Temple, which is one of the best structures loot-wise in the game. Having a high possibility of high tier enchantlets, and tons of enchanted books guaranteed to be protection 3.
